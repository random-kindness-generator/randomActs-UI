Regularly Practice Kindness

Sometimes life gets so busy that we forget to practice kindness and compassion. That's why we created Goodwill Gestures to send us daily reminders.

Build Better Connections

Goodwill Gestures takes daily reminders one step further by randomly selecting one of your friends to receive your act of kindness.

Kindness Spreads

Bringing joy to others spreads positivity to their relationships, too.

Improve your own Wellbeing

Giving and receiving acts of kindness increase your oxytocin, which is a hormone that helps lower blood pressure, increases self-esteem, and reduces feelings of depression.
